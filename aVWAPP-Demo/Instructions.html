aVWAPP (a Vulnerable Web Application) is a hackable online store to teach the basics of web exploitation and hacking. To get started, read the instructions and then click HERE.

Instructions

The point of aVWAPP is to exploit the online store in such a way to inject JavaScript, bypass item limits, and hack price forms. By doing these practice exercises, an understanding of HTML, insecure elements, and basic web attacks will be learned.
